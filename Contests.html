How to Enter

Submit your artwork by filling out the form below. Please make sure to read the rules and guidelines before submitting.

Prizes

1st Place: $500
2nd Place: $300
3rd Place: $200

Judging Criteria

Entries will be judged based on creativity, originality, and adherence to the theme.

Rules and Regulations

  • Participants must be 18 years or older.
  • Each participant can submit up to two artworks.
  • Artwork must be original and not previously published.
  • Artwork must be submitted in digital format.
